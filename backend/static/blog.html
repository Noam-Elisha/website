Cluster Chord Ear Training Tool

January 27, 2025

noamelisha.com/Cluster-Chords

Part of conductor training is ear training, picking apart chords and identifying all the notes in them, even when they're very dissonant. To train this we usually need two of us and one of us sits at the piano and the other faces away and guesses what notes are in the chords. They're called cluster chords because they use dissonant notes that sound like they're played right next to each other on the keyboard.

This tool I made helps practice ear training by yourself. It plays a chord for you and you have to mark the notes on the piano keyboard. Then you can check your answer and it'll tell you how many notes you got correct.
