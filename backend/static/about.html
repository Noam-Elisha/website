Hello, I'm Noam

I am a violinist/conductor/photographer/programmer. On the side I like to play guitar and write songs or rock/jazz-y kinds of musical doodles. I currently work as a software engineer for the NHERI SimCenter at the University of California, Berkeley, where I also went to school. I have an M.S. in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, B.A. in Music, B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and a B.S. in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research .

I was born in Israel, and between ages 1 and 9 lived in Belgium before moving to California where I live today. I speak English, French, and Hebrew and I am working on learning German.
